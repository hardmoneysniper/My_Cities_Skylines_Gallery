Novel City (Personal)

This city started out as a personal project from Nov 2020 and briefly completed at June 2023. It was forced to stop as the save hit most physical limits of the game. I spent the 1,000th hour on the game while playing the save.


City Stats:


  • 600,000 population, x10 in reality
  • Decentralized and well-connected multi-downtown mode
  • 100+ Public Transit Routes of subway, commuter rail, bus, streetcar, ferry, cablecar, airship, etc.
  • Over 1,000 units of ped-only roads

Guangzhou (Team member)

This project is a tribute and restoration of the city of Guangzhou in China. Located by the Pearl River, it is four of the Tier I cities in China, and the economic engine of the entire Pearl River Delta.

City Facts:


  • Over 8,000 assets are used to replicate the city. Some assets are custom-made as 1:1 replicas of landmarks in Guangzhou.
  • The city and all the assets was created by a team of 10 enthusiasts with different societal roles.
  • The official media of the Communist Party of Guangdong Province even noticed this work and used it as a propaganda.

Bayview (Under Construction)


The latest project as a personal project that I am working on. The city is settled on a beautiful coastline with mountains around.

Applying TOD (Transit-oriented Development) on city building and apply flexible zoning schemes to vitalize the community at the maximum extent, this city is an experiment to what is learned through the Urban Technology program.
